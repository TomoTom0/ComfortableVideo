# Privacy Policy for Comfortable Video

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2025

## Overview

Comfortable Video is a browser extension that enhances your video viewing experience by maximizing videos and providing smart controls. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Comfortable Video:
- Does **NOT** collect any personal information
- Does **NOT** track your browsing history
- Does **NOT** transmit any data to external servers
- Does **NOT** use analytics or tracking tools
- Does **NOT** share any information with third parties

## Permissions Used

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

### 1. `activeTab`
- **Purpose:** To access and modify the current tab's video elements when you activate comfort mode
- **Usage:** Only activated when you explicitly click the extension icon or use the context menu
- **Data:** No data is collected or stored; modifications are temporary and local

### 2. `contextMenus`
- **Purpose:** To add a right-click menu option for toggling comfort mode
- **Usage:** Adds a menu item "Toggle Comfort Video Mode" to the browser's context menu
- **Data:** No data is collected or stored

### 3. `storage`
- **Purpose:** To save your extension preferences locally on your device
- **Usage:** Stores user preferences (e.g., default settings) in your browser's local storage
- **Data:** All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted anywhere

### 4. `<all_urls>` (Content Scripts)
- **Purpose:** To detect and modify video elements on any website
- **Usage:** The extension scans for HTML5 video elements and applies styling/controls when comfort mode is activated
- **Data:** No data is collected; all operations are performed locally in your browser

## How the Extension Works

1. **Video Detection:** Scans the current page for HTML5 `<video>` elements
2. **Style Modification:** Temporarily modifies CSS to maximize video display
3. **Custom Controls:** Adds temporary control buttons for playback, seeking, etc.
4. **Local Operation:** All operations happen entirely within your browser

## Data Storage

The only data stored is:
- User preferences for the extension (stored locally in your browser)
- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No video viewing data

All stored data can be cleared by:
1. Removing the extension
2. Clearing your browser's extension data

## Third-Party Services

Comfortable Video does **NOT** use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools.
